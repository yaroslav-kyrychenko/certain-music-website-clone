About the artist
PYLOT
188,220 monthy listeners

Take a journey into the captivating world of electronic music, where the remarkable PYLOT reigns as a visionary artist. Since starting this project in 2015, PYLOT has become known for pushing boundaries and leaving a lasting impression in the electronic music scene. By blending elements of Synthwave and cyberpunk with finesse and attention to detail, PYLOT creates mesmerizing and immersive soundscapes that are filled with beautiful synth melodies and energizing rhythms. This talented artist has developed a unique style that can be both powerful and thought-provoking, capturing the hearts of listeners worldwide. PYLOT's creativity doesn't stop at the music itself. Each release comes with a special treat for fans—an original artwork and a captivating story. Through these visual and narrative elements, PYLOT brings a cinematic quality to the listening experience, giving fans the opportunity to dive deeper into the artist's world through the episodes and stories available on PYLOT's website. This fusion of music, art, and storytelling creates a truly engaging and unforgettable journey for fans to enjoy. Join PYLOT on an adventure where music and imagination intertwine: https://www.iampylot.com
